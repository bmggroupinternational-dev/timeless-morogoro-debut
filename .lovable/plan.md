

## Timeless Morogoro Hotel Website

A luxury, warm-toned hotel website with gold accents, elegant serif fonts, and rich earth colors matching the brand logo.

### Design System
- **Colors**: Deep gold (#B8860B), warm ivory (#FFFDF5), charcoal (#2C2C2C), soft cream (#F5F0E6)
- **Typography**: Serif headings (Playfair Display), clean sans-serif body text
- **Style**: Full-width hero images, generous whitespace, subtle gold dividers, elegant hover animations

### Pages & Sections

**1. Home Page**
- Full-screen hero with hotel tagline overlay and CTA button
- Welcome section with brief about text and elegant imagery
- Featured rooms preview (3 cards)
- Dining & amenities teaser
- Gallery preview strip
- Footer with contact info, social links, and quick navigation

**2. About Page**
- Hotel story and heritage
- Location highlights (Morogoro, Tanzania)
- Team/hospitality philosophy section

**3. Rooms Page**
- Visual room cards with photos, descriptions, amenities icons, and pricing
- "Inquire via WhatsApp/Email" button on each card

**4. Gallery Page**
- Masonry-style photo gallery with lightbox view
- Category filters (Rooms, Dining, Grounds, Views)

**5. Dining & Amenities Page**
- Restaurant section with menu highlights
- Spa, pool, and other facilities with imagery
- Operating hours and details

### Shared Components
- Sticky navigation bar with logo and gold accent links
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Smooth scroll animations on section entry
- Footer with contact details, location map link, and social media
